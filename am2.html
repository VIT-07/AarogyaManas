About

Arogya Manas was formed back then when we were given a problem statement in our Design Thinking lecture. We started the project unknowning of the fact that mental stress faced by the athletes was a serious and a severe problem. We realied this when we took interviews of athletes and coaches. The response we got from the interviewees was a genuine, authentic and sometimes were first hand experiences. We realized that mental stress was a serious issue and such an issue that should be treated immediately specifically for athletes. We here at Arogya Manas, will try to tackle your problems of mental stress. Driven by a team of passionate individuals — comprising mental health professionals, technology experts and the idea of a revolutionary mental care site was born. Our vision is simple yet profound: to create a digital sanctuary where individuals could find solace, support, and guidance on their journey toward mental wellness. And also, the genesis of the mental care site was not just a beginning, but a beacon of hope — a reminder that, even in the darkest of times, there are always hands reaching out, hearts willing to listen, and a world waiting to embrace those in need.
